Upgrade to WFL Pro

Why Upgrade?

When you're just starting with weight training, it's good to get experience with the basic lifts but to keep things relatively simple. After about 6-9 months of linear progression though, the basic training models lose their effectiveness and it becomes necessary to introduce more complexity into your training. WFL Pro was designed to facilitate a transition to more complex strength programming models. After upgrading, you will have access to the following features:

  • Unlimited Custom Exercises
  • Unlimited Workout Cycles
  • 1RM Calculator
  • Stats

Cycles can be used to represent a workout routine, or a macrocycle. If you are just about to start the Texas Method intermediate routine, for instance, you should create a new cycle in WFL to represent that phase of your training. This will help keep your workouts organized as you may need look at your training history down the road. Then, you can add any additional exercises to the app that you will need for that training period. Some programs require that you calculate the weight on the bar as a percentage of your one-rep max (1RM), so I am including a calculator to assist with that. Finally, the stats page will help you to see your overall progress on each lift. If you're still not sure, I urge you to at least try it out for a month and you can cancel your subscription at any time. I mean, who can't afford $2? You can always cancel at anytime by navigating to the account menu on the top-right.
