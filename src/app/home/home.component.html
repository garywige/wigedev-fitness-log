What does WFL do?

WFL is a fitness logging app designed to make planning and documenting your resistance training workouts a breeze. WFL is designed to work well both on mobile and desktop browsers. We utilize a dark theme to optimize battery life on mobile devices. You will find the calendar-based interface to be enjoyable to use.

Why do I need it?

Well, the truth is you don't need this app, or any app for that matter, to log your workouts. You could log your workouts just fine using a pen and paper. As a fitness enthusiast myself, I understand how important it is to have a workout journal, even if it is just a pen and paper. Excel also lends itself well to be used as a fitness log if you are already familiar with using it. But these methods aren't ideal. Who wants to carry a journal to the gym in this day & age? WFL negates the need for you to bring more than your mobile device to the gym while also being intuitive to use.
